What is full-funnel marketing?

The marketing funnel represents the buyer's journey from initial brand discovery and awareness, education and content engagement, through to conversion and purchase.

Top-, mid-, and bottom-funnel display advertising strategies each play distinct roles in the marketing funnel with specific objectives and metrics, working in harmony to make a big marketing impact..

Top of the funnel: reach precise business audiences wherever they are online to increase awareness and recall of your company and products. The goal is to convey what your company does, why your products are unique, and why prospects should care about your company.

Mid-funnel: educate, and influence prospects after they are aware of your brand, but before they are necessarily ready to engage with a sales rep - or before they are even ready to share their personal information (e.g., via a Web form).

Bottom of the funnel: convert prospects into leads and customers. With the awareness-building and nurturing work you have done higher up in the funnel, you should now have a healthy, growing pool of target prospects that are ready to share information, check out your free trial and possibly engage with your sales team.

Business Demographic Targeting

Target precise audiences online using business demographic data such as job function and seniority on over 120 million business professionals, enabling you to reach your target audiences with precision, wherever they travel across the business web.

Facebook Targeting

Combine B2B audience data with Facebook's incredible scale to reach your target business audiences on Facebook, and precisely target them based on B2B-focused criteria such as title, seniority, industry, company size, and more.

LinkedIn Targeting

Put your brand, messages, and offers in front of key decision makers, influential professionals, and affluent consumers on LinkedIn. Connect with your prospects while they network, and precisely target LinkedIn members based on criteria such as title, seniority, industry, and more.

Website Retargeting

Target highly relevant display ads to prospects after they leave your website, landing pages, or corporate blog so you can bring "lost" prospects back, boosting lead conversion rates across your mix.

CRM Retargeting

Keep your brand, messages, and offers in front of your existing prospects, beyond their inbox. By targeting online display ads to leads you've already captured in your CRM or Marketing Automation system, this retargeting strategy gives you a powerful new way to nurture the highly qualified audiences already in your sights.

Keyword Targeting

Combine search with display to reach audiences with display ads after they've performed a search using specific key terms. Catch your target audiences while they're thinking about relevant topics to make a timely impression.

Top-funnel success metrics:

Brand Recall: brand studies reveal the effect that display advertising has on your audience's familiarity with your brand.
Share of Voice: your brand's exposure to a specific business audience relative to other advertisers who are bidding on the same audience.
Targeted Reach: how many unique professionals in a specific target audience that you have access to via display advertising.
Impressions Delivered against Target Audience: how many unique display ads are served to your target audience online."

Mid-funnel success metrics:

Branded Search Lift: shows how display advertising impacts the number of website visitors who search specifically for your brand name in search engines.
New Visitors: the number of new visitors that visit your website after being exposed to your display ads.
Target Audience time on Site Lift: the increase in time spent on your website by your target audience.
Page Views/Visits: the impact that display advertising has on the number of visits or page views on your website.
Lift in Page Views per Visit: the increase in pages viewed per visit to your website as a result of display advertising.

Bottom-funnel success metrics:

Conversion Lift: display advertising's impact on website conversion rates.
Leads: the number of leads generated by display advertising.
Lead Quality: the quality of leads generated by display advertising.
Opportunity Contribution: how many display advertising sourced leads turn into sales opportunities.
Revenue Contribution: the amount of revenue directly attributable to display advertising
